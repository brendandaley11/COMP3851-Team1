About Us

What exactly is YoUon, and what do we do?

Who we are.

YoUon is an orientation website used to help new students get to know their university, and be a place to have all information they need to get started.
We know the struggle of giong into university with no knowledge, so this website is designed by students, for students, to help you navigate around.
We will help you get to know your university, what you need to get done straight away, what to expect during your time here, and more.

What matters to us

University life can be hard, and we have learned first hand the struggle that can come with it. Trying to work your way around campus, or the university
can be a daunting task. There are a lot of paths you can take, and very little information of where it will take you. Pages like MyHub can get confusing
very quickly, and can lead to a very bad university experience. We also know that making friends can be difficult as an adult. All of these and more are
the reason we made this site, to help new students understand what the uni is about, and to help them navigate uni life.

What we do

We help students through a variety of ways. We have tutorials on how to navigate pages like MyHub, to help you enroll in classes or to understand fees.
There also tutorials for navigating sites like blackboard, how to navigate campus, how to talk to your course coordinators, etc. These tutorials are in
both written and video form, and are quick and simple to understand. Everything we learned within the first couple of semesters should be learned once you
first start, so that's what we have done.
